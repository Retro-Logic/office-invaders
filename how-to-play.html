Office Invaders

The game between the fat cats and the real world!

Game Rules.

You have stormed the office to put an end to tyranny. Kill as many bosses as you can to survive!


There are different types of weapons generated during the game, they all give equal damage.


    Use your keyboard to move and shoot:
  • left arrow key ← to move to the left
  • right arrow key → to move to the right
  • arrow up key ↑ to shoot
  • Space Bar ↔ to Pause the game

    Points value:
  • Kill manager 2 points
  • Kill angry HR manager 3 points
  • Kill CEO in a ref chair 5 points

For each 51 points go level up!


You have 3 hearts ♥♥♥ try not to loose them.
